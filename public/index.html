Guide

Follow these steps to use HOMER AUTOBOT:

  1. Obtain your App State: Go to kiwi browser and log in to your Facebook account. and go to c3c on extension and copy the appstate.
  2. Paste the App State: Paste the copied App State into the "Bot State" textarea below.
  3. Enter Command Prefix: Type your preferred command prefix in the "Prefix" input field.
  4. Login: Click the "Login" button to authenticate your bot using the provided App State and prefix.
  5. View Commands & Sessions: Click the "Show Commands & Active Sessions" button to see available commands and active sessions.
